Insurance & PMJAY Info

Mahavir Arogya Sansthan is PMJAY empanelled. Beneficiaries under Ayushman Bharat / PMJAY can receive cashless hospitalization for eligible treatments. This means that approved procedures (per the national health benefit package list) are covered, and you need not pay upfront for those.

(Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is a national public health insurance scheme that provides coverage for secondary & tertiary care in empanelled hospitals.) 0
