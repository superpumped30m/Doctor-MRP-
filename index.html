Will showing prices reduce our margins?

Transparent pricing builds trust and reduces friction at billing. Most hospitals use tiered pricing (ward category, consumables) and display representative prices with notes. You stay in control of final billing.

What if competitors copy our prices?

Being first builds reputation and media interest. Patients reward clarity. You can periodically update prices and highlight quality, accreditation, and outcomes that competitors cannot copy.
